Master Mind

Break The Hidden Code

Select your colors and press the button to break the code!



*The rules are simple: The WOPR (War Operation Plan Response) computer (you can call him "Joshua") will select a random nuclear launch code made up of 4 colors (selected from the 6 colors available). Colors may be duplicated. You will have 10 guesses to break Joshua's code. After each guess, Joshua will provide feedback on your code's accuracy using the 4 blank spaces on the right - A black space means you have chosen the correct color and the correct position. A white space means you have chosen the correct color but placed it in the wrong position. A blank space indicates the wrong color was chosen. If you succeed in breaking Joshua's code within 10 guesses, you win the game! Joshua will return to DEFCON 5 and disarm all nukes. If you fail to guess the code by the 10th guess, Joshua wins the game...all nukes will be launched at their predetermined targets. Choose wisely...
