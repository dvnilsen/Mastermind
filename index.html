*GAME RULES:
War Games is a twist on the classic game of MasterMind. The WOPR computer (War Operation Plan Response - you can call him "Joshua") will select a random nuclear launch code made up of 4 colors (selected from the 6 colors available). Note: Colors may be duplicated. You will have 10 guesses to break Joshua's code. First click on the color you wish to use, then click on the bottom space that corresponds to the column you wish to place that color in. After each guess, Joshua will provide feedback on your code's accuracy using the 4 blank squares to the right of your guess - A red square means you have chosen the correct color and the correct position. A yellow square means you have chosen the correct color but placed it in the wrong position. A blank square indicates the wrong color was chosen. If you succeed in breaking Joshua's code within 10 guesses, you win the game! Joshua will return to DEFCON 5 and disarm all nuclear missles. If you fail to guess the code by the 10th guess, Joshua wins the game...all nulear missles will be launched at their predetermined targets. Think carefully. Choose wisely...
